Pancakes!

Description

Simple homemade pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Step: Mix flour, milk, egg, butter, sugar, baking powder, and salt together.
  2. Step: Heat a lightly oiled griddle over low heat. Scoop 1/4 cup batter onto the griddle and cook until top and edges are dry, 3 to 4 minutes.
  3. Step: Flip and cook until lightly browned on the other side, 2 to 3 minutes. Repeat with remaining batter.
  4. Enjoy your pancakaes!
