Grilled cheese sandwich!

Description

The classic grilled cheese sandwich!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 slices of cheese

Steps

  1. Step: Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet.
  2. Step: Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up).
  3. Step: Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted.
  4. Enjoy this classic homemade sandwich!
